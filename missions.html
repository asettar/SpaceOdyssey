Missions

Apollo 11, launched by NASA on July 16, 1969, aimed to land the first humans on the Moon and return them safely to Earth.

Curiosity, launched by NASA on November 26, 2011, explores Mars to study its climate, geology, and habitability.

Artemis I, launched by NASA on November 16, 2022, tested the Orion spacecraft for future crewed Moon missions.
